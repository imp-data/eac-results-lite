NESO EAC Auction Results – EFA Day Extract

This tool retrieves publicly available EAC auction data directly from the NESO Data Portal using their open APIs. All processing occurs entirely within your browser — no data is stored or processed on any server. You can explore the official datasets at the NESO EAC Auction Results page: https://www.neso.energy/data-portal/eac-auction-results .

EAC auction results are typically published around 3:00 PM UK local time. If you use the “Today’s Results” button before publication time, the output may be empty — in that case, please use the “Yesterday’s Results” button instead. Likewise, the quick‑link ranges (Last 7, 15, or 30 EFA Days) always count backwards from tomorrow’s EFA day end, but if the most recent EFA day has not yet been published, the returned range may contain one fewer day than expected. This simply reflects the publication schedule.
